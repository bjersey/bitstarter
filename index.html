We've all been there before: lunchtime in the office and faced with the same old options of some mega-chain fast food restaurant to another. Why force another cardboard sandwich down your throat when you can eat at a delicious, greasy, cheap, unique, and probably unhealthy food truck?

What it is: FoodMobile is the web and mobile app for food trucks. It's kind of like Google but with real time food truck information, all bundled in a nice little package to satisfy your hunger. With this app, users will be able to find food trucks near their location and get to know stuff like whether or not they're open, how delicious their food is, if they're out of napkins, and other general useful stuff.
Who it's for: Foodies and casual eaters alike. Basically anyone who doesn't mind eating every once in a while (you know you want some Korean BBQ tacos... like, right now).
